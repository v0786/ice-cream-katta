About Us

Ice Cream Katta is a friendly neighborhood spot built around one simple idea: make every scoop feel special. We focus on consistency, clean flavors, and a vibe that keeps you coming back.

Our Story

Ice Cream Katta started as a small counter serving classic scoops and thick shakes. Over time, we added crowd-favorite sundaes, seasonal specials, and take-home packs—without compromising on taste.

Whether you’re celebrating or just craving something sweet, our goal is to make it quick, smooth, and memorable—from the first look to the last bite.
